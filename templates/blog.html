18th March 2023/Fisheries How to get your fish active?

Successful fish health management begins with prevention of disease rather than treatment. Prevention of fish disease is accomplished through good water quality management, nutrition and sanitation. Without this foundation it is impossible to prevent outbreaks of opportunistic diseases. The fish is constantly bathed in potential pathogens, including bacteria, fungi, and parasites. Even use of sterilisation technology (ie, ultraviolet sterilisers, ozonation) does not eliminate all potential pathogens from the environment. Sub-optimal water quality, poor nutrition or immune system suppression generally associated with stressful conditions allow these potential pathogens to cause disease.
